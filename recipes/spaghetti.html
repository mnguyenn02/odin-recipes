spaghetti

This is spaghetti with bacon,cheese,and pasta in a spicy beef and tomato sauce. It can be cooked in a cast iron skillet.

  • 6 strips of bacon
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  1. Place bacon in pan and cook over medium-high heat
  2. add onion to same pan and saute until softened
  3. add garlic, salt, and pepper
  4. add ground beef and cook for about 5 minutes
